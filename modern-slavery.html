Modern Slavery and Human Trafficking Statement

Financial Year: 2025

Our Commitment: WFY Solutions is committed to preventing modern slavery and human trafficking in all forms within our business operations and supply chain. We have zero tolerance for slavery, servitude, forced labor, and human trafficking.

Introduction

This statement is made pursuant to Section 54 of the UK Modern Slavery Act 2015 and the California Transparency in Supply Chains Act of 2010. It outlines the steps WFY Solutions has taken and continues to take to ensure that modern slavery and human trafficking are not taking place in our business or supply chains.

Our Organization

WFY Solutions is a professional services firm specializing in AI automation, business intelligence, and machine learning solutions. We operate primarily as a remote consulting business with limited physical supply chains, focusing on technology services and intellectual deliverables.

Our Business Structure:

  • Sole proprietorship based in Morrisville, Vermont
  • Remote operation with minimal physical infrastructure
  • Technology and services-based business model
  • Limited direct supply chain exposure

Our Policies

Zero Tolerance Policy:

We maintain a zero-tolerance approach to modern slavery and human trafficking. This applies to:

  • Direct employment and contractors
  • Business relationships and partnerships
  • Third-party service providers
  • Technology vendors and suppliers

Ethical Business Practices:

  • Commitment to fair labor practices
  • Respect for human rights and dignity
  • Transparent and ethical business conduct
  • Compliance with all applicable labor laws

Due Diligence Processes

Supplier Assessment:

While our direct supply chain is limited, we conduct due diligence on:

  • Technology platform providers (cloud services, software vendors)
  • Professional service contractors and consultants
  • Any third-party suppliers of goods or services

Risk Assessment:

  • Evaluation of supply chain risks
  • Review of supplier labor practices
  • Assessment of geographical risk factors
  • Monitoring of high-risk industries or sectors

Supplier Responsibility

We expect all suppliers and contractors to:

  • Comply with all applicable laws regarding modern slavery and human trafficking
  • Maintain ethical labor practices throughout their operations
  • Provide safe and fair working conditions
  • Pay fair wages that meet or exceed minimum wage requirements
  • Respect workers' freedom of association and collective bargaining rights
  • Prohibit the use of forced, bonded, or involuntary labor
  • Prohibit the use of child labor

Training and Awareness

We are committed to raising awareness about modern slavery and human trafficking:

  • Understanding of modern slavery indicators
  • Recognition of potential risks in business relationships
  • Procedures for reporting concerns or suspicions
  • Regular updates on relevant legislation and best practices

Reporting Mechanisms

We encourage the reporting of any concerns related to modern slavery or human trafficking:

  • Reporting Channels: Concerns can be reported via our LinkedIn profile or direct communication
  • Protection: No retaliation for good-faith reports
  • Investigation: All reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly
  • Confidentiality: Reports are handled with appropriate confidentiality

Key Performance Indicators

We measure the effectiveness of our efforts through:

  • Number of supplier assessments completed
  • Percentage of suppliers acknowledging our standards
  • Number of training sessions conducted
  • Response time to reported concerns
  • Remediation actions taken when issues are identified

Our Commitment to Continuous Improvement

We are committed to:

  • Regularly reviewing and updating our policies
  • Enhancing due diligence procedures
  • Expanding training and awareness initiatives
  • Collaborating with industry partners on best practices
  • Staying informed about emerging risks and legislation

Technology Sector Considerations

As a technology-focused business, we recognize specific risks in the tech supply chain:

  • Electronics manufacturing and raw material sourcing
  • Data center and infrastructure operations
  • Offshore development and outsourcing practices
  • Content moderation and gig economy workers

We actively consider these factors when selecting technology partners and platforms.

Remote Work Considerations

Operating as a remote business, we are mindful of:

  • Fair treatment of remote workers and contractors
  • Transparent payment and working arrangements
  • Clear contractual terms and conditions
  • Respect for work-life boundaries

Collaboration and Industry Standards

We support and align with:

  • United Nations Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights
  • International Labour Organization (ILO) standards
  • Tech Against Trafficking initiatives
  • Responsible Business Alliance Code of Conduct

Transparency and Accountability

This statement demonstrates our commitment to transparency regarding our efforts to combat modern slavery and human trafficking. We:

  • Publish this statement annually
  • Make it readily accessible on our website
  • Update it to reflect our evolving practices
  • Take responsibility for our actions and commitments

Remediation

If we identify instances of modern slavery or human trafficking:

  • We will take immediate action to address the situation
  • Work with affected parties to provide support and remediation
  • Terminate relationships with non-compliant suppliers if necessary
  • Report to appropriate authorities as required by law
  • Implement measures to prevent recurrence

Looking Forward

We recognize that combating modern slavery and human trafficking is an ongoing effort. We commit to:

  • Continuously strengthening our policies and procedures
  • Increasing engagement with suppliers on these issues
  • Enhancing transparency in our operations
  • Contributing to broader industry efforts to eliminate modern slavery

Approval and Review

This statement has been approved by William Young, Principal of WFY Solutions, and will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to reflect our ongoing commitment and any changes in our business or practices.
